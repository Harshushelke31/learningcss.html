Mayoori was born to a Tamil couple in Kolkata, in 1983. She landed a lead role in the Tamil film Kumbakonam Gopalu (1998), opposite Pandiarajan, when she was in Class 8. Despite her young age, she did a splendid job as a nurse in the film. (Image: News18 Hindi)
